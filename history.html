Company History

Penneagle Golf was founded in 2002 by Timothy Durnin. The company's goal was to develop a line of putters that enable golfers to putt more consistently, lower their number of Putts Per Round and spend less time on the GREEN.

Penneagle developed mallet and blade putters have a traditional design and feel that utilizes advanced technology and optical enhancing alignment guides to maximize performance. The putter head is made of CNC precision milled 304 stainless steel with a satin finish to ensure premium quality and durability. Our putter designs conform with United States Golf Association (USGA) rules "relating to the design and manufacturer of golf clubs".

The first Penneagle TPD (Tour Proven Design) MALLET models (Palisade, Stinson) and Highland Blade were introduced in 2004. The Penneagle BLADE models (Carmel and Atherton) were introduced in 2008 and the Palisade 2 Mallet in 2009 The Penneagle TPD putter models feature our patented OPPOSITIONAL WEIGHTING SYSTEM (OWS) and patented PENNEAGLE ALIGNMENT GUIDES (I-Ball & V-Site) that aid in making putting more dependable, consistent and accurate for golfers at all levels.

In 2020, Chase Durnin bought out the company in his passion and love for golf. He hopes to put a modern spin on Penneagle and revive the impeccable design of Penneagle putters. Penneagle will live up to the highest standards so every golfer will be proud to carry our putters in their bag.
